This method creates lasting comprehension rather than temporary memorization.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Newton's Adda different from other coaching centers?

Newton's Adda focuses on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning. Our unique teaching methodology, developed by Yogesh Pandey (M.Sc. Physics), emphasizes real-world applications and problem-solving techniques that help students develop a deeper understanding of physics principles.

What class levels do you cater to?

We offer comprehensive programs for:

  • Class 9–10 (Foundation Program)
  • Class 11–12 (Advanced Program)

Do you offer both online and offline classes?

Yes, we provide flexible learning options:

  • In-person classes at our Prayagraj center
  • Live online classes with interactive features
  • Recorded sessions for revision

How are the batches structured?

We maintain small batch sizes (15–20 students max) to ensure personalized attention. Batches are organized based on:

  • Class/grade level
  • Learning pace (regular vs. advanced)
  • Exam preparation needs

What study materials are provided?

Students receive:

  • Comprehensive chapter notes
  • Practice problem sets with solutions
  • Previous year question papers
  • Concept maps and formula sheets
  • Access to our digital library

How can I assess my child's progress?

We provide multiple progress tracking mechanisms:

  • Weekly quizzes with performance analysis
  • Monthly comprehensive tests
  • Detailed progress reports
  • Regular parent-teacher meetings
  • 24/7 access to performance dashboard
